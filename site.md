## 🏛️ About

National AI Technologies is Oman's flagship sovereign AI platform, developed to localize advanced digital technologies and drive the digital transformation of the Sultanate's economy.

The platform serves as the **digital backbone** for:

- Government institutions
- Private enterprises
- Academic organizations

Enabling them to harness AI while maintaining **full data sovereignty** within Omani borders, stimulating innovation across health, education, economy, and environment sectors.
